About Grovon

Your partner in advanced growth

Grovon is an innovative, technology‑driven growth partner specializing in empowering organizations with advanced sales enablement, intelligent process outsourcing, process automation, and digital transformation solutions. We help global enterprises accelerate revenue, optimize client acquisition, and scale operations efficiently while delivering measurable business outcomes through AI‑powered tools, data‑driven insights, and scalable service models.

We prioritize seamless customer experiences and operational excellence that drive sustainable growth and competitive advantage in today’s digital‑first marketplace.

Sales Operations Manager

You’ll drive forecasting, pipeline health, resource planning, CRM integrity, and sales process compliance while improving enablement and team productivity.

What we’re looking for: 8+ yrs in sales ops/management, analytics, CRM & telephony expertise, cross‑functional collaboration, stellar communication.

Sales Team Lead

Based at: Regional Office  |  Aligned with: Sales Operations Manager  |  Leads: 10‑12 CAEs

Coach and manage a high‑performance team to exceed targets, ensure pipeline quality, and uphold CRM & compliance standards.

What we’re looking for: 5+ yrs leadership in recruitment/tech sales, funnel analysis, communication, negotiation, resilience.

Quality Lead

Based at: HQ  |  Aligned with: Head of Operations

Define and improve quality standards across sales and service delivery. Lead audits, compliance, RCA, and corrective actions.

What we’re looking for: 5–7 yrs in quality management (BPO/KPO), process audits, frameworks, communication, and training.
